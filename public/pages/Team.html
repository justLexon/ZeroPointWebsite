About US

Zero Point is a rising force in the competitive esports world, with our current focus on making a major impact in Apex Legends. Our team is built on dedication, skill, and a relentless drive to compete at the highest level. We believe in fostering a strong team spirit, strategic gameplay, and continuous improvement to climb the ranks and establish ourselves among the elite.

But our vision goes beyond just one game. As we solidify our presence in Apex Legends, we are actively exploring opportunities to expand into other competitive titles, aiming to build a versatile and respected multi-game organization. Whether it's shooters, strategy games, or MOBAs, Zero Point is committed to excellence and adaptability.

We value our fans, partners, and the esports community, working together to grow and elevate the competitive scene. Join us on our journey as we chase greatness, challenge the best, and represent the pinnacle of esports passion and professionalism.
